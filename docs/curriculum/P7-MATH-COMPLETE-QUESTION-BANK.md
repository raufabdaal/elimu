# Canonical P7 Mathematics (NCDC & UNEB PLE) Complete Question Bank

This canonical document contains the exhaustive, progressive multi-module question bank for **Primary 7 Mathematics (`p7-math`)** in Elimu. It covers 100% of all 8 core NCDC curriculum categories tested in the Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB) Primary Leaving Examinations (PLE), broken down into **9 Master Topics** across **27 Bite-Sized Modules (`329 Progressive Questions`)** with strict Ugandan context (`UGX, Owino Market, Nakasero, Kasese, Soroti, Gulu, Mbarara`) and zero repetitive numbers.
