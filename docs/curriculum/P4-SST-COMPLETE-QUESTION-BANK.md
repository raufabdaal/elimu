### 2. Weather, Seasons & Vegetation in Our District (`p4-sst-weather-vegetation`) - 3 Modules, 36 Questions
- **Module 1: Weather vs Climate & Weather Measuring Instruments** (`p4-wv-m1`, 12 Questions)
  - Weather (`short-term daily atmospheric condition`) vs Climate (`30-35 year average`); UNMA meteorology; Rain Gauge (`mm at 09:00 AM, sunk $30\text{ cm}$ above ground in open grass`); Wind Vane (`direction FROM`) vs Anemometer (`speed km/h/knots`); Thermometer (`Six's Max/Min in $^\circ\text{C}$`); Stevenson Screen (`louvred white box $1.2\text{ m}$ high facing away from sun/South`); Hygrometer (`Relative Humidity %`); Campbell-Stokes Sunshine Recorder (`hours`); Barometer (`atmospheric pressure mb`); Wind sock (`points downwind/East means Westerly wind`); Diurnal Range vs Mean Daily Temperature.
- **Module 2: The Two Seasons (Wet vs Dry) & Human Farming Activities** (`p4-wv-m2`, 12 Questions)
  - Wet season (`heavy rain, high humidity - farmers clear, plow, sow seeds, weed, fertilize`) vs Dry season (`bright sun, low humidity - farmers harvest, winnow, sun-dry on tarpaulins/racks below $12\%$ moisture stopping Aflatoxins`); Double Maxima bimodal rain (`southern Lake Victoria basin March-May and Sept-Nov`) vs Unimodal single maxima (`Karamoja April-Aug $<500\text{ mm}$ nomadic pastoralism`); Fodder preservation (`sun-dried Hay vs fermented green Silage`); Rainwater harvesting against droughts; Weather hazards (`Hailstorms tree windbreaks, Flash floods swamp protection, Droughts valley dams, Wind gales shelterbelts/propping matooke`); Irrigation (`Doho/Mubuku schemes`); Rotational Grazing / Paddocking (`preventing overgrazing/soil erosion and breaking tick cycles`); Mosquito stagnant pools hygiene.
- **Module 3: Natural vs Planted Vegetation & Stopping Deforestation** (`p4-wv-m3`, 12 Questions)
  - Natural vs Planted/Artificial vegetation; Tropical Savannah Grasslands (`over $60\%$ area, elephant grass, acacia/baobab pyrophytes/deciduous`); Mabira Rainforest (`hardwood mahogany/mvule, buttress roots, lianas`); Deforestation hazards (`destroying water cycle transpiration, topsoil erosion, local droughts`); Afforestation vs Re-afforestation (`'Cut one tree, plant three more'`); Agroforestry (`Calliandra, Sesbania, Grevillea fixing Rhizobium nitrogen and providing cow fodder without drying water tables like Eucalyptus`); NFA central forest reserves (`506 CFRs, transit hammer stamps`); 4-stage mountain deforestation chain reaction (`tree removal -> splash/sheet erosion -> landslides/siltation -> drought/famine`); Energy-saving smokeless stoves (`Sigiri/Lorena cutting wood use $50\%$ against indoor carbon monoxide smoke`); Shea Butter Tree (`Vitellaria paradoxa Moo-ya cultural taboo protection`).
