# Canonical P6 Mathematics (NCDC Curriculum) Complete Question Bank

This canonical document contains the exhaustive, progressive multi-module question bank for **Primary 6 Mathematics (`p6-math`)** in Elimu. It covers 100% of all 9 canonical Uganda National Curriculum Development Centre (NCDC) syllabus units, structured into **9 Master Topics** across **28 Bite-Sized Modules (`342 Progressive Questions Total`)** with strict Ugandan context (`zero repetitive questions, authentic P6/PLE multi-step reasoning, NCDC vocabulary, and exact past-paper alignment`).

---

## Complete Topic & Module Roadmap (`9 Topics`, `28 Modules`, `342 Questions`)

### 1. Set Theory & Venn Diagrams (`p6-math-sets`) ŌĆö 3 Modules (`36 Questions`)
- `p6-sets-m1`: Module 1: Types of Sets, Subsets & Power Sets (`12 questions`: finite, infinite, empty/null set `{} or ├ś`, equal vs equivalent sets, finding subsets formula $2^n$ and proper subsets $2^n - 1$).
- `p6-sets-m2`: Module 2: Set Operations & Venn Diagram Shading (`12 questions`: intersection `A Ōł® B`, union `A Ōł¬ B`, complement `A'`, universal set `╬Ą`, shading regions and listing elements).
- `p6-sets-m3`: Module 3: 2-Set Venn Diagram Word Problems (`12 questions`: real-life survey word problems: e.g. pupils liking Matooke vs Rice, finding intersection $x$ using equations, finding those liking only one subject or neither).

### 2. Whole Numbers, Place Values & Number Bases (`p6-math-numbers`) ŌĆö 3 Modules (`37 Questions`)
- `p6-num-m1`: Module 1: Place Values, Total Values & Rounding Off (`12 questions`: place values up to billions, writing large numbers in words and figures, rounding off to nearest tens, hundreds, thousands, and ten thousands).
- `p6-num-m2`: Module 2: Large Arithmetic Operations & Roman Numerals (`12 questions`: addition/subtraction/long division of 5 and 6 digit numbers, Roman numerals up to $M$ (`I, V, X, L, C, D, M`), converting $1984 = MCMLXXXIV$ and vice versa).
- `p6-num-m3`: Module 3: Number Bases (`Base Two & Base Five`) (`13 questions`: converting numbers from base ten to base two/five and vice versa (`e.g., 25 ten = 11001 two; 34 five = 19 ten`), addition and subtraction in base two and base five).

### 3. Number Patterns, Sequences & Prime Factorization (`p6-math-patterns`) ŌĆö 3 Modules (`37 Questions`)
- `p6-pat-m1`: Module 1: Prime Factors, Square Roots & Divisibility Rules (`12 questions`: prime numbers vs composite, factor trees and index notation $2^3 \times 3^2$, finding square roots using prime factorization, divisibility rules for 3, 4, 6, 8, 9, 11).
- `p6-pat-m2`: Module 2: Lowest Common Multiple (LCM) & Greatest Common Factor (GCF) (`12 questions`: finding GCF and LCM of 2 and 3 numbers, siren/bell repeating interval problems (`LCM`), dividing exercise books equally (`GCF`)).
- `p6-pat-m3`: Module 3: Arithmetic, Square & Triangular Number Sequences (`13 questions`: finding missing terms in arithmetic sequences, triangular numbers $T_n = \frac{n(n+1)}{2}$, consecutive odd/even number word problems).

### 4. Fractions, Decimals & Percentages (`p6-math-fractions`) ŌĆö 4 Modules (`49 Questions`)
- `p6-frc-m1`: Module 1: Vulgar & Mixed Fractions with BODMAS (`12 questions`: equivalent fractions, comparing/ordering fractions using LCM of denominators, operations on mixed fractions using BODMAS, sharing land/harvest fraction word problems).
- `p6-frc-m2`: Module 2: Decimals & Place Value Operations (`12 questions`: place values up to thousandths, converting fractions to decimals and vice versa, terminating vs recurring decimals, decimal multiplication and division).
- `p6-frc-m3`: Module 3: Percentage Conversions & Proportions (`12 questions`: converting fractions/decimals to percentages and vice versa, expressing one quantity as a percentage of another e.g. 45 minutes of 2 hours, finding percentages of quantities).
- `p6-frc-m4`: Module 4: Percentage Increase, Decrease & Discount Word Problems (`13 questions`: school enrollment increasing by 20%, price drops during Owino clearance sales, finding original price before tax or discount).

### 5. Integers & Number Lines (`p6-math-integers`) ŌĆö 2 Modules (`24 Questions`)
- `p6-int-m1`: Module 1: Number Line Representation & Addition/Subtraction (`12 questions`: positive/negative/zero on horizontal number lines, ordering integers $-15 < -4 < +7$, evaluating $-8 + (-5) - (-12)$, temperature drops on Mt. Elgon).
- `p6-int-m2`: Module 2: Multiplication, Division & Real-Life Integer Word Problems (`12 questions`: sign rules $+ \times - = -$, evaluating $(-6) \times (-4) \div (-3)$, bank overdraft balances $-150,000\text{ UGX}$ plus deposits, submarine diving altitudes below sea level).

### 6. Algebraic Expressions & Equations (`p6-math-algebra`) ŌĆö 3 Modules (`38 Questions`)
- `p6-alg-m1`: Module 1: Simplifying Expressions, Like Terms & Substitution (`12 questions`: variables vs coefficients, collecting like terms $6a - 2b + 3a + 5b$, expanding brackets $3(2y - 4) - 2(y - 5)$, evaluating $2x^2 - 3y$ when $x=4, y=-2$).
- `p6-alg-m2`: Module 2: Solving Linear Equations & Inequalities (`13 questions`: solving one-step and two-step equations $3m - 7 = 14$, fractional equations $\frac{2x+1}{5} = 3$, bracket equations $4(p - 2) = 2(p + 3)$, inequality solution sets on number lines).
- `p6-alg-m3`: Module 3: Formulating & Solving Word Equations (`13 questions`: "Think of a number, double it, and add 6 to get 30", consecutive numbers whose sum is 75, rectangular garden perimeter with algebraic sides $2x+4$ and $x+3$, age ratio equations).

### 7. Angles, Triangles, Circles & Mensuration (`p6-math-geometry`) ŌĆö 4 Modules (`48 Questions`)
- `p6-geo-m1`: Module 1: Types of Angles, Complementary & Supplementary Angles (`12 questions`: acute, right, obtuse, straight, reflex angles; complementary angles sum $= 90^\circ$, supplementary angles sum $= 180^\circ$, vertically opposite angles, angles around a point sum $= 360^\circ$).
- `p6-geo-m2`: Module 2: Parallel Lines & Triangle Properties (`12 questions`: alternate Z-angles, corresponding F-angles, co-interior C-angles; equilateral, isosceles, right-angled triangles, Pythagoras theorem $a^2 + b^2 = c^2$ e.g. 3-4-5 and 6-8-10 triangles).
- `p6-geo-m3`: Module 3: Perimeter & Area of 2D & Composite Shapes (`12 questions`: perimeter and area of rectangles $L \times W$, squares $s^2$, triangles $\frac{1}{2} b h$, parallelograms, and trapeziums $\frac{1}{2}(a+b)h$; dividing composite L-shapes into rectangles).
- `p6-geo-m4`: Module 4: Circles, Cubes, Cuboids & Cylinders (`12 questions`: radius, diameter, circumference $C = \pi d$, area of circle $A = \pi r^2$ where $\pi = \frac{22}{7}$; volume and surface area of cubes $s^3$, cuboids $L \times W \times H$, and cylinders $\pi r^2 h$).

### 8. Statistics, Bar Graphs, Pie Charts & Probability (`p6-math-data`) ŌĆö 3 Modules (`36 Questions`)
- `p6-dat-m1`: Module 1: Mean, Median, Mode & Range (`12 questions`: calculating mean average of test scores, finding missing marks when mean is known, median of odd and even datasets, mode of frequency tables, range $= \text{Highest} - \text{Lowest}$).
- `p6-dat-m2`: Module 2: Bar Graphs, Line Graphs & Pie Charts (`12 questions`: drawing and interpreting vertical/horizontal bar graphs and temperature line graphs across Kampala/Soroti; pie chart central angles $\frac{\text{Angle}}{360^\circ} \times \text{Total}$, calculating pupil counts from right-angle sectors $90^\circ$).
- `p6-dat-m3`: Module 3: Basic Probability & Simple Outcomes (`12 questions`: likelihood scale from impossible ($0$) to certain ($1$); probability of rolling prime numbers on a 6-sided die ($\frac{1}{2}$), picking colored marbles from a bag, complementary events $P(A) + P(A') = 1$).

### 9. Business Mathematics, Time, Speed & Rates (`p6-math-business-time`) ŌĆö 3 Modules (`37 Questions`)
- `p6-bus-m1`: Module 1: Money, Profit, Loss & Percentage Profit/Loss (`12 questions`: UGX currency, buying and selling, $\text{Profit} = \text{Selling Price} - \text{Cost Price}$, $\text{Loss} = \text{Cost Price} - \text{Selling Price}$, percentage profit $\frac{\text{Profit}}{\text{Cost Price}} \times 100\%$, shopping bill change calculations).
- `p6-bus-m2`: Module 2: Simple Interest & Time Clock Conversions (`12 questions`: calculating Simple Interest $I = \frac{P \times R \times T}{100}$ for 1, 2, and 3 years; converting between 12-hour clock (`AM/PM`) and 24-hour clock (`00:00 to 23:59`), time addition and subtraction (`borrowing 60 minutes`), bus timetables).
- `p6-bus-m3`: Module 3: Distance, Speed, Time & Average Speed (`13 questions`: $\text{Distance} = \text{Speed} \times \text{Time}$, $\text{Speed} = \frac{\text{Distance}}{\text{Time}}$, $\text{Time} = \frac{\text{Distance}}{\text{Speed}}$; converting $\text{km/h}$ to $\text{m/s}$ (`multiply by 5/18`), average speed across two-stage journeys ($\frac{\text{Total Distance}}{\text{Total Elapsed Time}}$)).
