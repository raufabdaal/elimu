# Canonical P7 Social Studies (NCDC & UNEB PLE) Complete Question Bank

This canonical document contains the exhaustive, progressive multi-module question bank for **Primary 7 Social Studies (`p7-sst`)** in Elimu. It covers 100% of the 5 canonical Uganda National Curriculum Development Centre (NCDC) and Primary Leaving Examinations (PLE) syllabus units (`Geography of Africa`, `History & People of Africa`, `Political & Economic Developments with all 6 Country Case Studies`, `Regional & Global Organizations`, and `Integrated Religious Education CRE & IRE`), structured into **7 Master Topics** across **21 Bite-Sized Modules (`262 Progressive Questions Total`)** with strict Ugandan & African context and zero repetitive questions.

---

## Complete Topic & Module Roadmap (`7 Topics`, `21 Modules`, `262 Questions`)

### 1. Our Country Uganda: Physical Features, Governance & Economy (`p7-sst-uganda`) — 3 Modules (`39 Questions`)
- `p7-uganda-m1`: Module 1: Location, Position & Physical Features (`12 questions`: latitude/longitude of Uganda, Equator $0^\circ$, Rift Valley, lakes and rivers).
- `p7-uganda-m2`: Module 2: Economy, Minerals & Tourism (`12 questions`: cash crops, copper/gold/oil minerals, tourism as invisible export, UWA).
- `p7-uganda-m3`: Module 3: People, Culture, Towns & Early History (`15 questions`: Bantu/Nilotic migrations, pre-colonial kingdoms, Local Council LC1–LC5 system).

### 2. Africa: Physical Features, Climate & Natural Vegetation (`p7-sst-africa-physical`) — 3 Modules (`37 Questions`)
- `p7-afp-m1`: Module 1: Location, Position & Major Mountains of Africa (`12 questions`: Equator/Tropic of Cancer/Tropic of Capricorn, Mt. Kilimanjaro volcanic dome, Mt. Rwenzori horst/block mountain, Atlas fold mountains).
- `p7-afp-m2`: Module 2: Major Rivers, Lakes & Drainage Systems of Africa (`12 questions`: River Nile from Lake Victoria to Mediterranean, Victoria Falls on River Zambezi, River Congo crossed twice by Equator, rift valley lakes vs down-warped lakes).
- `p7-afp-m3`: Module 3: Climate Zones, Natural Vegetation & Wildlife Conservation (`13 questions`: Equatorial rainforests canopy/lianas, Savannah grasslands and wildlife migrations, Mediterranean climate, xerophytic desert adaptations, Bwindi Impenetrable National Park).

### 3. The People & History of Africa: Migrations, Explorers & Colonization (`p7-sst-africa-people`) — 4 Modules (`49 Questions`)
- `p7-afh-m1`: Module 1: Early Ethnic Migrations & Pre-Colonial Kingdoms (`12 questions`: Bantu iron-working/farming, River-Lake Nilotes Luo from Bahr-el-Ghazal, Bunyoro-Kitara Empire under Bachwezi, Shaka Zulu military cow-horn formation).
- `p7-afh-m2`: Module 2: Foreign Explorers, Missionaries & Arab Traders (`12 questions`: John Speke discovering source of River Nile 1862, Henry Morton Stanley's 1875 letter for Kabaka Mutesa I, Alexander Mackay/CMS introducing printing/hospitals, Uganda Martyrs executed at Namugongo by Kabaka Mwanga).
- `p7-afh-m3`: Module 3: Scramble for Africa, Colonial Systems & African Resistance (`12 questions`: Berlin Conference 1884–1885, British Indirect Rule vs French Assimilation, 1900 Buganda Agreement Mailo land, Omukama Kabalega guerrilla resistance, Maji Maji rebellion).
- `p7-afh-m4`: Module 4: Independence Movement & African Nationalists (`13 questions`: Dr. Apollo Milton Obote leading Uganda independence on 9th October 1962, Sir Edward Mutesa II, Jomo Kenyatta of Kenya, Julius Nyerere of Tanzania, Kwame Nkrumah of Ghana, Nelson Mandela overthrowing Apartheid).

### 4. Economic Development & International Organizations: EAC, AU & UN (`p7-sst-world`) — 3 Modules (`37 Questions`)
- `p7-org-m1`: Module 1: Economic Activities & Resources across Africa (`12 questions`: subsistence vs commercial plantation farming, coffee/tea/cocoa cash crops, Zambia Copperbelt and South Africa gold/diamonds, value addition and hydroelectric power dams along River Nile).
- `p7-org-m2`: Module 2: Regional Economic Blocs: EAC, COMESA & IGAD (`12 questions`: East African Community EAC headquarters in Arusha, 8 partner states, Customs Union/Common Market/Monetary Union/Political Federation, COMESA and IGAD peace/drought mandates).
- `p7-org-m3`: Module 3: Continental & Global Governance: AU, UN Agencies & Commonwealth (`13 questions`: African Union AU headquarters in Addis Ababa founded 2002, United Nations UN founded 1945, specialized agencies in Uganda: UNICEF, WHO, UNHCR, WFP, FAO; Commonwealth of Nations and CHOGM).

### 5. African Economic Case Studies (`p7-sst-cases`) — 3 Modules (`36 Questions`)
- `p7-case-m1`: Module 1: West African Case Studies - Ghana & Nigeria (`12 questions`: Ghana's Cocoa Triangle, Akosombo Dam / Lake Volta aluminum smelting at Tema; Nigeria's petroleum in Niger Delta / Port Harcourt, OPEC membership, palm oil, Abuja capital & ECOWAS HQ).
- `p7-case-m2`: Module 2: Southern & Central African Case Studies - South Africa & DR Congo (`12 questions`: South Africa's Rand gold in Witwatersrand, Kimberley diamond Big Hole, coal/platinum, Western Cape Mediterranean fruit/wine farming; DR Congo's Katanga Copperbelt, cobalt for electric vehicle batteries, equatorial timber, Inga Dams on River Congo).
- `p7-case-m3`: Module 3: North & East African Case Studies - Libya & Egypt/Sudan River Projects (`12 questions`: Libya's petroleum wealth, Great Man-Made River underground Saharan aquifer pipe network; Egypt's Aswan High Dam / Lake Nasser, perennial irrigation / shadoof; Sudan's Gezira Scheme growing long-staple cotton between Blue and White Nile).

### 6. PLE Christian Religious Education - CRE Section (`p7-sst-cre`) — 3 Modules (`38 Questions`)
- `p7-cre-m1`: Module 1: God's Creation, The Fall of Man & Old Testament Patriarchs (`12 questions`: 6 days of creation in Genesis, human environmental stewardship, Adam & Eve / origin of sin, Noah's covenant rainbow, Abraham father of faith, Moses & the Ten Commandments at Mt. Sinai, Joseph forgiving his brothers).
- `p7-cre-m2`: Module 2: The Life, Ministry, Parables & Miracles of Jesus Christ (`13 questions`: birth in Bethlehem, baptism by John in River Jordan, Beatitudes / Sermon on the Mount, parables: Good Samaritan, Prodigal Son, Sower; miracles: feeding the 5,000, raising Lazarus, turning water to wine at Cana; Great Commandment of Love).
- `p7-cre-m3`: Module 3: Holy Week, The Early Church, Sacraments & Christian Living Today (`13 questions`: Palm Sunday entry into Jerusalem, Last Supper / Holy Communion on Holy Thursday, Good Friday crucifixion at Golgotha, Easter Sunday resurrection, Pentecost Holy Spirit descent upon apostles, Uganda Martyrs heroism at Namugongo, Christian forgiveness & charity).

### 7. PLE Islamic Religious Education - IRE Section (`p7-sst-ire`) — 2 Modules (`26 Questions`)
- `p7-ire-m1`: Module 1: The Pillars of Islam, Articles of Faith & Holy Scriptures (`13 questions`: Five Pillars: Shahada declaration of faith, Salat five daily prayers facing Kaaba, Zakat 2.5% wealth tax, Sawm Ramadan fasting, Hajj Mecca pilgrimage; Six Articles of Faith: belief in Allah, Angels like Jibril/Mikail/Israfil, Holy Books Qur'an/Tawrat/Zabur/Injil, Prophets, Day of Judgment, Divine Decree Qadar).
- `p7-ire-m2`: Module 2: The Life of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH), Islamic Ethics & Uganda Muslim History (`13 questions`: birth in Mecca 570 CE, first revelation in Cave Hira, Hijra migration to Medina in 622 CE, Islamic ethics forbidding alcohol/pork/backbiting/Riba, Sadaqah daily charity; history of Islam in Uganda: arrival of Arab traders like Ahmed bin Ibrahim around 1844, Kabaka Mutesa I, Prince Nuhu Mbogo, Uganda Muslim Supreme Council UMSC at Old Kampala).
