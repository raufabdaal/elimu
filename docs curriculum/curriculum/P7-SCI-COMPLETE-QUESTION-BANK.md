# Canonical P7 Integrated Science (NCDC & UNEB PLE) Complete Question Bank

This canonical document contains the exhaustive, progressive multi-module question bank for **Primary 7 Integrated Science (`p7-sci`)** in Elimu. It covers 100% of all 8 canonical Uganda National Curriculum Development Centre (NCDC) and Primary Leaving Examinations (PLE) syllabus units, structured into **8 Master Topics** across **27 Bite-Sized Modules (`332 Progressive Questions Total`)** with strict Ugandan context (`zero repetitive questions, authentic PLE multi-step reasoning, NCDC vocabulary, and exact UNEB past-paper alignment`).

---

## Complete Topic & Module Roadmap (`8 Topics`, `27 Modules`, `332 Questions`)

### 1. The Human Muscular & Skeletal System (`p7-sci-muscular-skeletal`) — 3 Modules (`37 Questions`)
- `p7-msk-m1`: Module 1: Structure & Functions of the Human Skeleton (`12 questions`: axial vs appendicular skeleton, cranium protecting brain, ribcage protecting heart/lungs, red bone marrow hemopoiesis, calcium/phosphorus mineral storage).
- `p7-msk-m2`: Module 2: Types of Joints, Ligaments & Antagonistic Muscles (`12 questions`: ball-and-socket shoulder/hip joints vs hinge elbow/knee joints, pivot/suture joints, ligaments vs tendons, biceps/triceps antagonistic pairs, cardiac muscle).
- `p7-msk-m3`: Module 3: Posture, Diseases & First Aid of the Skeletal System (`13 questions`: Polio viral paralysis / OPV drops, Rickets Vitamin D/calcium deficiency bowed legs, simple/compound/greenstick fractures, splints and slings, R.I.C.E. for sprains).

### 2. Electricity & Magnetism (`p7-sci-electricity`) — 3 Modules (`37 Questions`)
- `p7-ele-m1`: Module 1: Simple Electric Circuits & Components (`12 questions`: dry cell chemical-to-electrical energy, copper wire, switch/key, series loops vs parallel house wiring, fuse/circuit breaker).
- `p7-ele-m2`: Module 2: Conductors, Insulators & Static Lightning Discharge (`12 questions`: good conductors copper/silver/salty water vs insulators rubber/plastic/dry wood, static discharge lightning, lightning arrestors grounded in moist earth).
- `p7-ele-m3`: Module 3: Properties & Uses of Magnets and Electromagnets (`13 questions`: freely suspended magnet North-South alignment, like poles repelling while unlike attract, ferromagnetic iron/cobalt/nickel, stroking vs electrical magnetization, demagnetizing via heating/dropping/AC, soft iron electromagnets in bells/cranes).

### 3. Sound, Light & Optical Instruments (`p7-sci-light-sound`) — 4 Modules (`49 Questions`)
- `p7-lso-m1`: Module 1: Production, Propagation & Reflection of Sound (`12 questions`: vibrations of strings/tuning forks/drum skins, requires a medium / fastest in steel / cannot travel through a vacuum, pitch vs volume/amplitude, echoes in bat/dolphin echolocation and ship SONAR).
- `p7-lso-m2`: Module 2: Rectilinear Propagation & Reflection of Light (`12 questions`: light traveling in straight lines / pinhole cameras, luminous vs non-luminous objects, regular reflection $i = r$ vs diffuse reflection, periscopes, umbra vs penumbra shadows).
- `p7-lso-m3`: Module 3: Refraction, Dispersion & The Rainbow Spectrum (`12 questions`: bending of light across medium density boundaries / apparent vs real depth, glass prism dispersion of white light into ROYGBIV spectrum, natural rainbow, primary colors Red/Green/Blue).
- `p7-lso-m4`: Module 4: The Human Eye, Camera & Optical Eye Defects (`13 questions`: cornea, pupil, iris, retina inverted image, optic nerve; comparing eye parts to camera shutter/film; short-sightedness/myopia corrected with concave lenses vs long-sightedness/hypermetropia corrected with convex lenses; cataracts, trachoma, night blindness).

### 4. The Human Excretory System & Skin Hygiene (`p7-sci-excretory`) — 3 Modules (`37 Questions`)
- `p7-exc-m1`: Module 1: The Kidneys & Urinary System (`12 questions`: biological definition of excretion vs egestion, bean-shaped kidneys filtering urea/salts/water, renal artery/vein, nephron cortex/medulla, ureters, urinary bladder, urethra; nephritis, kidney stones, dialysis).
- `p7-exc-m2`: Module 2: The Skin as an Excretory & Temperature Regulating Organ (`12 questions`: outer epidermis and inner dermis, sweat glands/pores excreting sweat, evaporative cooling on hot days vs hair erector muscles on cold days, melanin, sebaceous glands/sebum, ringworm, scabies, leprosy).
- `p7-exc-m3`: Module 3: The Lungs, Liver & Excretory System Hygiene (`13 questions`: lungs excreting carbon dioxide/water vapor; liver excreting bile pigments, deaminating amino acids to form urea, and detoxifying poisons/alcohol; hepatitis, cirrhosis, lime water test for carbon dioxide, avoiding tobacco/smoking).

### 5. The Human Circulatory System (`p7-sci-circulatory`) — 3 Modules (`38 Questions`)
- `p7-cir-m1`: Module 1: Structure & Functions of the Human Heart (`12 questions`: four chambers: right/left auricles and ventricles; left ventricle having thickest wall to pump blood to entire body via aorta; tricuspid, bicuspid, and semi-lunar valves; septum preventing mixing; cardiac muscle and coronary arteries; stethoscope).
- `p7-cir-m2`: Module 2: Blood Vessels & Circulation Pathways (`13 questions`: arteries carrying blood away under high pulse pressure with thick elastic walls vs veins carrying blood back with thin walls/wide lumen/valves; pulmonary artery deoxygenated vs pulmonary vein oxygenated; microscopic capillaries; double circulation: pulmonary vs systemic loops).
- `p7-cir-m3`: Module 3: Components of Blood, Clotting & Circulatory Diseases (`13 questions`: plasma, red blood cells with hemoglobin, white blood cells phagocytosis/antibodies, platelets clotting via fibrinogen/thrombin/fibrin mesh scab, pulse rate 72 bpm, hypertension, sickle cell anemia, hemophilia, blood groups O universal donor vs AB universal recipient, lymphatic system).

### 6. Tropical Health, Diseases & Vectors (`p7-sci-health-vectors`) — 4 Modules (`49 Questions`)
- `p7-vec-m1`: Module 1: Mosquito-Borne Diseases - Malaria, Yellow Fever & Dengue (`12 questions`: female Anopheles transmitting Plasmodium protozoa causing fever/chills/joint pains; sleeping under ITNs, draining stagnant pools, oil on water to suffocate larvae wrigglers; Aedes aegypti and yellow/dengue fever; complete metamorphosis).
- `p7-vec-m2`: Module 2: Tsetse Fly, Housefly & Water-Borne Sanitation Diseases (`12 questions`: Tsetse fly (`Glossina`) transmitting Trypanosoma sleeping sickness in humans and Nagana in cattle / bluetraps; Housefly (`Musca domestica`) mechanical transmission of cholera, typhoid, and dysentery; exact ORS/SSS recipe: 8 spoons sugar + 1 spoon salt in 1 liter boiled cooled water; VIP latrines).
- `p7-vec-m3`: Module 3: Bilharzia, Rabies & Other Tropical Vectors (`12 questions`: Bilharzia/schistosomiasis transmitted via freshwater water snails (`Bulinus/Biomphalaria`) when bathing in lakes; Rabies from dog/jackal bites; River Blindness from Blackfly (`Simulium`) in fast river rapids; ticks and East Coast Fever; jiggers and lice).
- `p7-vec-m4`: Module 4: Sexually Transmitted Infections (STIs) & HIV/AIDS Prevention (`13 questions`: HIV destroying CD4 white blood cells leading to AIDS and opportunistic infections; modes of spread: unprotected sexual contact, sharing unsterilized needles/razor blades, mother-to-child; abstinence as the only 100% effective method for P7 pupils; syphilis, gonorrhea, ARV adherence).

### 7. Interdependence of Things & Ecosystems (`p7-sci-ecosystem`) — 3 Modules (`36 Questions`)
- `p7-eco-m1`: Module 1: Photosynthesis & Biotic/Abiotic Interdependence (`12 questions`: green plants manufacturing glucose using carbon dioxide, water, sunlight, and chlorophyll while releasing oxygen; autotrophs vs heterotrophs/decomposers; iodine test for starch).
- `p7-eco-m2`: Module 2: Food Chains, Food Webs & Trophic Levels (`12 questions`: arrows showing flow of energy; producers, primary consumers/herbivores, secondary consumers/carnivores, tertiary predators, and decomposers/fungi; 10% energy transfer law; scavengers, omnivores, biomagnification).
- `p7-eco-m3`: Module 3: Symbiotic Relationships & Environmental Conservation (`12 questions`: mutualism in Rhizobium nitrogen-fixing bacteria in bean nodules or oxpecker birds; parasitism in tapeworms/ticks or dodder plants; commensalism in epiphytic orchids; deforestation, swamp reclamation, agroforestry, terracing, mulching, compost making, NEMA).

### 8. Agriculture, Farm Management & Apiculture (`p7-sci-agriculture`) — 4 Modules (`49 Questions`)
- `p7-agr-m1`: Module 1: Soil Science, Soil Types & Components (`12 questions`: loam soil ideal for crops due to humus and balanced drainage; clay soil with microscopic particles and high capillarity used for pottery/bricks; sand soil with fast drainage; earthworms aerating soil; soil profile horizons A, B, C, D).
- `p7-agr-m2`: Module 2: Soil Erosion Types & Conservation Methods (`12 questions`: splash, sheet, rill, and gully erosion; terracing steep hillsides, mulching, contour plowing, strip cropping, cover cropping, check dams, rotational grazing, crop rotation, green manure).
- `p7-agr-m3`: Module 3: Cattle Management & Poultry Farming (`12 questions`: indigenous/local Ankole long-horn and Zebu resistant to ticks/heat vs exotic Friesian for milk and Aberdeen Angus for beef; rotational grazing, paddocking, dipping acaricides against East Coast Fever; layers vs broilers; Newcastle disease, coccidiosis, brooders, deep litter).
- `p7-agr-m4`: Module 4: Bee Keeping (Apiculture) & Honey Harvesting (`13 questions`: Queen bee laying eggs and queen substance, Drone fertile males mating during nuptial flight, Worker infertile females performing 100% of hive labor; Kenya Top Bar KTB hive vs log hive; smoker, protective overall, extracting ripe capped honeycombs, waggle dance).
